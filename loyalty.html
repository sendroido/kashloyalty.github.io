Loyalty

At checkout, customers opt-in by entering their phone number directly from your point of sale (POS) terminal.*

Once done, everytime a customer spends money at your business, they earn points toward a reward you set — like a free coffee, or 20% off a purchase*

With point expiry reminders, customers are notified via SMS, 7 days and 3 days before their points expire so they keep coming back*
