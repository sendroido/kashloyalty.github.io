Reasons to Signup


  • Why do we need to link your card?

    So we can give you cash! We track your transactional data to reward you with cash on Messenger, so you don't have to carry extra cards or open new apps.


  • What information does Kash see from my linked card(s)?

    Kash only sees your transaction data. We never see your account balance or any other personal information linked to your card(s).


  • How are you able to access my transaction data without storing my card credentials?

    To access your transaction data, Paystack transforms your credentials into a key that is meaningless out of context, even to us.
