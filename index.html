Frequently asked questions

What does Kash do? What is a loyalty program?

It costs 25X more to acquire a new customer than it does to sell to an existing one. It makes sense to reward your customers each time they come into the store, so they keep coming back more and more often. Kash makes it simple to run a customer loyalty program by offering your customers rewards they can redeem after spending a certain amount.

How does a loyalty program benefit my business?

  • Your customers will feel happy when they receive and redeem rewards, encouraging them to come back to your business more regularly
  • Happy customers will come back more often and spend more, increasing your revenue
  • Happy, regular customers will generate word of mouth by recommending you to their friends and giving positive reviews, bringing you new customers to start the cycle again

Will Kash work for my store?

Yes! As long as you are a brick-and-mortar store, Kash will work for you. We have clients in many different industries (restaurants, spas, salons, retail, and more!)

My customers don’t have any tech expertise. Can they set up Kash?

Yes! No programming or tech needed. In fact, Kash is designed to be set up in just minutes. You can see how it works here.

How do I know what rewards to choose?

Setting the right reward depends on your preference, your store and your industry. We have a detailed guide on how to choose the best reward for your program.

I have more than 1 outlet. Can I use Kash?

Yup! Kash works its magic whether you have 1 store or 1,000. For multiple outlets, the setup fee is NGN25,000 for the first outlet, NGN10,000 for each subsequent one and NGN0 for unlimited customers.

Can I send unlimited SMSes to my customers?
